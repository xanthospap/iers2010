The IERS

If you want to find out more about the models and algorithms implemented in the libiers10++ package, visit the IERS website. This site also contains a lot of information and links, about various other resources (e.g. documents, technical notes, software, products, etc).

The IERS Conventions Center Home operated since 2001 by The Bureau International des Poids et Mesures (BIPM) and the US Naval Observatory (USNO) contains material describing reference systems realized by the IERS and the models and procedures used for this purpose.
